## Why

NeuroRouter is developed by Obsta Labs, LLC and uses an AGPL community edition plus commercial licensing for organizations that need different terms. To maintain this, all contributions must grant rights that allow both licensing tracks to coexist.

Without a CLA, outside contributions can only be distributed under the open-source license, which limits the project's ability to offer commercial terms.

## How to sign

Signing is automatic. When you submit a pull request, the CLA bot will check whether you have signed. If not, it will post instructions in the PR.

First-time contributors: comment `I have read the CLA and I agree` on your PR. This is recorded and covers all future contributions to this repository.
